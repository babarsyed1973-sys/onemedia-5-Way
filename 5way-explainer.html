LEVER 01 — anim-left d1
MORE
LEADS
More opportunities means more chances to win. But it doesn't require cold calling strangers — you likely have 230+ dormant accounts right now.
+10% = just 0.8 extra opps per month
across the whole year
DO THIS
Contact 5 existing dormant accounts per week — you have 230 waiting
DO THIS
End every call with: "What projects do you have coming up in 3 months?"
DO THIS
Use every manufacturer launch as a reason to call 50 accounts
DO THIS
Build a monthly call plan: top 30 accounts, every one touched each month
2
LEVER 02
BETTER
CONVERSION
Win more of the deals you already quote. We have 2,308 opps stuck at "Quoted" — many go cold because nobody follows up.
Win rate: 49.5% by count
but only 14.3% by value — fix the big deals
DO THIS
Follow up every quote within 48 hours — if you quoted it, chase it
DO THIS
Qualify before quoting: Budget? Timeline? Decision-maker? Don't waste effort on dead leads
DO THIS
Review your last 5 losses — write the reason down, find the pattern
DO THIS
Speed wins: same-day quotes on standard products. Be the fastest response
3
LEVER 03
MORE
TRANSACTIONS
Get each account ordering more often. Most accounts buy only 1–2 of our 12+ manufacturer lines — every new line is another transaction.
1,250 opps at "Pre-Stage Renewal"
waiting to be captured
DO THIS
Cross-sell: if they buy ONELAN, have you shown them MediaStar? Reserva? Datapath?
DO THIS
Own your renewals — flag licences due in 90 days and call before expiry, not after
DO THIS
30-day post-delivery check-in: "How did it go? What's the next project on site?"
DO THIS
Share project wins: "We just did a video wall for [X] — is that something your clients need?"
4
LEVER 04
BIGGER
DEAL SIZE
Our median deal is £1,851 but average is £5,934. Bundling and solution-selling lifts every deal closer to the average.
+£500 per deal — one extra product line
or services add-on
DO THIS
Bundle the solution: display + processor + mount + software + installation in one quote
DO THIS
Always add the onemedia services line — installation, commissioning, extended warranty
DO THIS
Show the "next step up": if they want 1 display, show how 3 work as a video wall
DO THIS
Understand the full project — not just the product they asked about
5
LEVER 05
PROTECT
MARGIN
Don't give away what you've earned. We had 114 credit notes totalling -£222K last year. Every 1% discount comes straight off the bottom line.
£10K deal at 20% = £2,000 profit
at 18% = £1,800. That £200 is gone forever
DO THIS
Lead with availability, expertise and support — not price
DO THIS
Never discount without getting something back: volume, commitment, or a case study
DO THIS
Check your buy price before quoting — are you on the best manufacturer tier?
DO THIS
Get orders right first time — every credit note and return destroys margin directly
